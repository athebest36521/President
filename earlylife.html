John Adams

Early Life

John Adams was born in 1735. His parents were John Adams Sr. and Susanna Boylston 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦. They lived in Massachusetts🌎. His dad was a farmer and shoemaker👨‍🌾. His mom's family had doctors👨‍⚕️. John went to school🎒. He even went to Harvard College 🏫. He decided to become a lawyer 👨‍⚖️.
John Adams became a leader. He wanted America to be free 🇺🇲. He helped start the country. He was important in the fight for independence. He was a smart and brave man.
